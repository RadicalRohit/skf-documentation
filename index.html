Active and Inactive a record

In a senario where you need to activate or disable a record, first select the record you wish to change the status. After selection, select or button and your record will be updated as per your selection.


button will change the record status to Active, by which the specific record(s) will be get functional or visible.
button will change the record status to Inactive, by which the specific record(s) will be not functional or visible.
After selection of any above opration a pop-up of comfirmation will appear.
